Introducing Fruit Mix

Fruit Mix Juice is thicker than normal fruit juice and contains more acidity and pilp. While you could drink it, the consistency is more of a puree than a juice.
Once water and sweeteners are added, you can drink fruit juice the way you would enjoy a glass of fruit juice.

Ingredients : Water, Fruit Pulp(Pineapple, Mango, Papaw, Banana), Sugar, Vanila, Salt and Fruit Mix Juice is made from Locally grown fresh fruits.
